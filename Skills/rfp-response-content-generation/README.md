# RFP Response - Content Generation

Drafts a complete proposal Word document section by section using the approved Strategy Brief as its blueprint, grounding every section in source documents before writing and inserting structured placeholders rather than inventing content where gaps exist.

![preview](./assets/preview.png)

## What you get

- Complete proposal draft Word document (.docx) saved to the deal subfolder
- Every section drafted in Adapt, Synthesize, or Placeholder mode based on available source material
- Mandatory-requirement register tracked and answered per section
- Exceptions & Assumptions section included
- Page budget reconciliation — flags over- and under-developed sections
- Firm position boundary enforced — no invented proof points or client names
